About Us

We are a group of professional tennis coaches who are passionate about promoting tennis as a lifestyle.

Coaches are Thomas, Robert and Ben. We have been playing tennis for more than 10 years and participated on several local tournaments for single and double.

We pride ourselves with ablilty to provide firstly fun tennis with hard working and dedication to excel that caters for all type of players. We have been trained in coaching techniques to deliver consistent results. Coaches vs students are kept to minimum to ensure enough coaching time for each students to cater for individual needs.

Hope that you enjoy this web site, even if you don't engage us for coaching. There are plenty of tennis tips and interesting facts on tennis for those who are passionate about tennis. Thank you again for visiting this side.

Our Values

Founded in 2010, we strive to develop world class tennis programme to provide improvement in skills and technique to achive mastery in the game of tennis.

We provide mini tournaments to encourage competitiveness amongst students and foster positive interest to improve their skils to the next level.

To tailor the needs for different groups or individual, we provide variaty of tennis programs that fit with envision the best quality through years of experience as group of coaches.
